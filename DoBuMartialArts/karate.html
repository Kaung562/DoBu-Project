Karate

Karate is a japanese martial art that originated on the island of Okinawa and is known for its striking techniques. It emphasizes using punches, kicks, knee strikes, elbow strikes, and open-hand techniques for both self-defense and combat sport. It has its roots in the indigenous fighting methods of Okinawa, which were influenced by Chinese martial arts.Over time, these local techniques evolved and were systmatized into what we know as Karate. It was further developed and spread during the 20th century. It places a strong emphasis on striking techniques executed with precision, speed, and power. Pratitioners learn various punches, kicks, knee strikes, and elbow strikes, each with specific applications and target.There are several different styles of karate, each with its own focus and variations in techiques. Beyond physical techiques, Karate places importance on discipline, respect, and mental development.Pratitioners not only how to defend themselves but also how to maintain self-control, focus and huminity.
